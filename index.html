01

Record automatically.

RiftMate detects when you enter champion select and starts capturing in the background. Stops when the game ends. No hotkeys, no OBS, no fiddling with bit-rates.

  • Hardware-accelerated H.264 encoding
  • Per-queue recording rules
  • Automatic disk management
02

Jump to any moment.

Every kill, death, dragon, baron, herald, turret and inhibitor lives on a synced timeline. Click an event — the video is already there.

  • Full objective and kill timeline
  • In-game bookmarks via hotkey
  • Frame-accurate scrubbing
03

Review what went wrong.

Filter the timeline to only show deaths. Spot the pattern — bad wards, overstaying, getting caught. Understand why you lost, not just that you did.

  • Objective-only review mode
  • Side-by-side KDA context
